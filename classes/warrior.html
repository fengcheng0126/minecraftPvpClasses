Warrior Class Evolutions

Basic: Warrior Class

Speed: Attack: Defence: Utility: Magic: Range: Healing:
2/5 3/5 2/5 1/5 1/5 Short 1/5

The warrior class is a versatile meelee troop, that can very easily deal damage to enemies. It wields a sword and has medium armor, such as chainmail peices. It is focused on damaging the opponent and does not really have tricks. It has a sword and a few other weapons and items. If you'd like to, you can give the warrior a potion of regeneration. As said, it is very versatile and can be used in pretty much any meelee combat situation. Try to go in the midst of battle with your Stone sword to get the best results.

Items:

  • Iron sword
  • 10 Steaks
  • Stone axe
  • Bucket of milk
  • 1 potion of regeneration
  • 1 potion of fire resistance
  • Chainmail Helmet
  • Chainmail Chestplate, Unbreaking I
  • Chainmail leggings

Tier 1: Knight

Speed: Attack: Defence: Utility: Magic: Range: Healing:
2/5 3/5 4/5 1/5 2/5 Short 1/5

The knight, a defensive upgrade of a warrior, is dressed for defence with iron armor and a shield but only an iron sword. Go defensive and stay at the edge of a brawl for your iron sword won't do much against the other classes. However, the knight plays the waiting game, with golden apples and iron armor. Plus, knights can have totems of undying to give extra hitpoints. Just slowly wear down your enemies and you can win. On a team, knights do the absolute best with an offensive class and ranged classes. Finally, if you are on a team of support and ranged classes, try to tank most of the damage so that the supports can help you with ranged attacks.

Items:

  • Iron sword
  • 2 Golden apples
  • Totem of undying
  • shield
  • 20 cobblestone
  • 10 Steaks
  • Full iron armor, Protection I for leggings and boots

Tier 1: Gladiator

Speed: Attack: Defence: Utility: Magic: Range: Healing:
3/5 4/5 1/5 0/5 2/5 Short to medium 0/5

The gladiator is a mainly offensive class that has more reach than the knight class. It should be noted that it has a strength potion so you can be a powerful fighter without needing much defence. Also, the gladiator has a leaping and swiftness potion for dodging, as the main form of defence. The weapon of the gladiator is a sweeping edge I diamond sword for the medium range on offence. If used in a team, the best combos would be a mage evolution, as they have Healing: potions as well as other effects. Another combo would be the gladiator-knight combo, as the knight can soak up damage while the gladiator attacks. You can not have any armor other than leather, but that does not stop you from dominating. The best tactic for the gladiator is to quickly damage, and then drop back to let the other teammate(s) pvp.

Items:

  • Diamond sword, sweeping edge II
  • 1 Strength potion, extended(8 minutes)
  • 1 Leaping potion, extended(8 minutes)
  • 2 swiftness II potions(1min 30secs)
  • Iron axe, Sharpness II
  • 10 Steaks
  • Leather chestplate
  • Leather boots, Frost walker I
