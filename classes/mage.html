Basic: Mage Class

Speed: Attack: Defence: Utility: Magic: Range: Healing:
3/5 3/5 2/5 2/5 3/5 medium 2/5

The mage is the underdog here. It has decent attack but bad defence. It has mostly potions to use and also blocks like magma. Play smart and cautious with your potions and use them smart.

Items:

  • 1 potion of strength(3 mins)
  • 2 splash potions of poison
  • 2 splash potions of weakness
  • 2 splash potions of harming
  • 1 lingering potion of regeneration
  • 2 potions of healing
  • 1 Totem of undying
